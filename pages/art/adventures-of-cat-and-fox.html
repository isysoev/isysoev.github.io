Adventures of the little cat and fox



Adventures of the little cat and fox started as little sketches that I drew to encourage my good friend, who was going through difficult times. An artist as well, my friend portrayed herself as a black winged cat. I wanted to draw myself in these sketches too, and I wondered: what animal should I be then? I loved the fox from The Little Prince, and I found the foxes' mix of curiosity and shyness very relatable. Thus I became a fox.

When I started the Adventures, I only knew how to do pencil drawings, but over time I ventured into acrylics, watercolors and digital art. My friend encouraged me to post the paintings, and they become my means to share some warmth, gentleness and playful spirit. I hope they can bring some delight to you as well!
